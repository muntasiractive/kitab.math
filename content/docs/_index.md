# Introduction

Welcome to **Kitab.Math**, an open-source documentation database dedicated to Mathematics.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, community-driven resource for learners and researchers of all levels, covering everything from basic arithmetic and algebra to advanced calculus, linear algebra, and discrete mathematics.

## Our Goal

To create a centralized, searchable, and collaborative knowledge base that empowers students and enthusiasts to understand the mathematical language that describes our universe.
